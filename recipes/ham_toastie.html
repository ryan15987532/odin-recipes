Ham Toastie

Description

A delicious recipe for a ham toastie. Add cheese to complement the dish. A quick and easy recipe, prepared in just minutes.

Ingredients

    Bread (2 slices)
    Butter
    Ham
    Cheese (optional)

Steps

    Evenly spread butter on both sides of each slice of bread
    Place 2 slices of ham on the buttered bread
    Optional Thinly cut cheese and place on top of the ham
    Put the second slice of bread on top to create a sandwich
    Put sandwich into George Foreman (or an alternative brand) and check on the toastie after 2 minutes
    If the toastie is toasted to your taste, remove the toastie and enjoy
