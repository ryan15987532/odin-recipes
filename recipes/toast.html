Toast

Description

A quick and easy recipe for toast, widely eaten for breakfast but also a quick snack for any time of day.

Ingredients

    2 slices of bread
    Butter

Steps

    Insert 2 slices of bread into your toaster
    After 1 minute check your toast, toast for longer if necessary
    Put your toast on a plate, evenly spread some butter over both slices
    Cut each slice in half and enjoy!
