Mamas Instant Noodles

Description

Mamas instant noodles are a staple food in South East Asia. A delicious snack, perfect a cheap and easy lunch or dinner.

Ingredients/requirements

    Mamas instant noodle pot
    Water
    A means of boiling water

Steps

    Boil water
    Peel back lid film from Mamas pot (dont remove it altogether)
    Remove flavour sachets and fork from noodles and set aside
    Carefully pour boiling water to fill line (about an inch from the top of noodle cup)
    Close the lid and weigh it down using the plastic fork
    Wait 5 minutes
    Remove lid and add flavour sachets, stir well and enjoy!
